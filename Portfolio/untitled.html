Introduction

Jose Maria has been making music since he was a young boy. Currently, he plays over nine instruments and is a singer/songwriter as well as an audio engineer.

Social Media

Jose Maria Fierro is present on many different forms of social media, and this is how he releases most of his music and communicates with the majority of his fans and friends.

Facebook: www.facebook.com/josefierro19

Youtube: www.youtube.com/josefierro19

Soundcloud: www.soundcloud.com/josefierro19

Twitter: @JoseMariaFierro

Works Done

Jose Maria has worked on many projects over the past years. Some notable ones include:

Recorded on and produced two CDs with StepFive Studios.

Featured on a track with BASS Adjustment (available on iTunes).

International collaboration with duo Nabil and Karim.

Featured on a cross generational hip hop track on the "Badasht: Visionaries" CD.

Multiple music videos done at different events and venues.
